Welcome to PSIT

Pranveer Singh Institute of Technology (PSIT), Kanpur, is one of the premier institutes in the country. Established in 2004, PSIT has been a pioneer in providing quality technical and management education.

Our mission is to achieve excellence in the field of technical education and research through education, innovation, and teamwork. We are committed to providing our students with a world-class education, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a vibrant campus life.
